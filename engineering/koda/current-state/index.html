What has been validated

Recorded validation includes interface and interaction gates, prototype accessibility behaviour, restart restoration, truthful live-versus-historical state presentation, optional observability comparison, and factory/recovery machinery. These outcomes establish their stated bounded results only.

What has failed or remains unresolved

The prior R1.1 data-identity and split problem was corrected in a regenerated corpus, but the resulting research-only candidate remains below its frozen qualification thresholds. A relationship visualisation is deferred until stable identity data exists. Some planned systems have no public implementation evidence. These are retained as visible constraints rather than hidden behind roadmaps.
