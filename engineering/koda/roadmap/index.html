Dependency-aware direction

Roadmap and end state

Koda's roadmap is a status map, not a release calendar. It records dependencies and boundaries without inventing dates or converting intent into delivery claims.

Long-term system vision

The intended end state is a local-first operating environment in which interfaces, models, bounded tools, evidence records and human decisions can coexist without obscuring authority or provenance. It is a direction of travel rather than a declaration that the environment already exists.

That end state depends on real, inspectable relationships between components. It cannot be reached by decorating incomplete connections or treating a visual map as proof of a working system.

What is intentionally not scheduled

No dates are assigned here. The current record does not support reliable delivery dates for the Shard Field, a mobile runtime, model training, research campaigns or a completed operating environment. Publication of a roadmap does not authorise those activities.
