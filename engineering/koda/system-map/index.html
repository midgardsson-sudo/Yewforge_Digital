Nivren System Map

A public technical map of Nivren’s interaction, orchestration, service, model, evidence, observability and research layers.

CURRENTLast reviewed: 15 July 2026Evidence classes: IMPLEMENTED · DESIGNED · PLANNED

Legend and reading boundary

Current implemented current boundary; Partially implemented bounded working evidence; Planned or dormant design or research boundary. Labels and text, not colour alone, convey status.

Conceptual map: this is not deployed network topology, active service topology, security architecture or evidence of a complete Nivren OS.

Layered operating environment

Nivren is organised as bounded layers: human authority; interaction surfaces; Workshop orchestration; specialist organs and services; local and research model work; evidence and state; observability; and dormant security research. A layer may be current, partial, planned or dormant without implying that all layers are active.
