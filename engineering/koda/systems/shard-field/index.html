Koda Shard Field Interaction Model

The sealed design direction and bounded prototype evidence for Koda’s future Shard Field interaction model.

PLANNEDLast reviewed: 15 July 2026Evidence classes: IMPLEMENTED · DESIGNED · PLANNED

Design maturity

Locked direction The Shard Field canon defines a unified object family, Field Memory, Constellation, Weight, Tide and Depth. It is a sealed design direction, not a complete runtime implementation.

Prototype and accessibility

Bounded HTML proof work demonstrated selected interaction concepts. The future field retains keyboard operation, reduced motion and non-window interaction grammar as requirements.

Current boundary

Core Shard is the present bounded prototype. Shard Field, Context Web, final interaction physics and a Koda-native application family remain unimplemented or separately gated.

Sources and provenance

Public-safe summary from canonical result records, design locks and the Engineering Journal source registers. Internal paths, startup details and sensitive topology are withheld.
